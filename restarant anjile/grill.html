Welcome to Cupcakes of Westdale Village, Hamilton’s first bakery devoted strictly to cupcakes. We offer a variety of gourmet cupcakes for all occasions. Weddings, showers, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, dinner parties or a simple afternoon treat…..cupcakes make any occasion more fun. Cupcakes make a wonderful edible gift. We have many gift packages from our single greeting boxes (better than a greeting card) to our gift trays (a nice alternative to sending flowers). At Cupcakes we are dedicated to helping you customize an order that will be perfect for you.
